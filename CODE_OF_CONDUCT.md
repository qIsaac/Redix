# Code Of Conduct

This project expects respectful, constructive collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and direct.
- Assume good intent.
- Keep technical discussions focused on the work.
- Respect that maintainers and contributors may have different time zones and availability.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.
